My Dearest Catherine,

There are moments in life that quietly rearrange everything — the way morning light shifts before sunrise, or the way a single melody can make the world stand still. That is what knowing you has been for me. Quietly, beautifully, and completely transforming.

You carry a warmth that I have found nowhere else — a kindness that isn't performed but simply exists in how you speak, how you listen, how you make everyone around you feel seen. In your presence, I have discovered a version of myself that I am proud to be.

I do not offer you perfect words, for no words are adequate. I offer you instead something truer: the certainty that every quiet thought I have eventually finds its way back to you. You are where my thoughts rest, where my hopes begin, and where my prayers are most sincere.

Thank you, Cathy, for existing in this world. And for letting me have the extraordinary privilege of existing in yours.

— Yours, always. Qassim 💌
